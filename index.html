Stories of Strength
& Transformation

Priya

Priya, a young mother from Semmencherry, lost her husband and had to raise two small children alone. With no stable income and growing expenses, she struggled to support her family while managing childcare.

USDT recognized Priya’s potential and provided her with a grinder to start a dosa batter business from home, allowing her to earn an income while caring for her children.

Priya now runs a successful dosa batter business, earning a stable income to support her children’s education and daily needs. This support has given her financial independence and confidence to move forward.
